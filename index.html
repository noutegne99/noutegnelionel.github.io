Lionel Noutegne

Lionel Noutegne is a computer engineer at WARWII-Solution in (Quebec) since september 2019. I defended M.Sc in Big data and Computer security in 2020 at the African Institut for mathematical Science (AIMS-Senegal), which I started in 2018 with Prof. Djiby Sow, as teacher-researcher at the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science of the Faculty of Science and Technology of Cheikh Anta Diop University (UCAD) in Dakar. My first M.Sc, was obtained in Data Mining in 2018 at the Yaounde 1 University, which I started in 2016 with Prof. Marcel Fouda Ndjodo. I obtained my B.Sc in fundamental science at the University of Douala .

At WARWII-Solution, i interested in software development and research in optimization problems and its application in the fields of satellite.
